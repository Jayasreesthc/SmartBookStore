About

Fluence

Fluence is a simple and user-friendly platform designed to streamline the process of managing books, customers, and orders. It allows administrators to add, update, and organize books efficiently, while providing customers with an easy way to browse, search, and purchase their favorite titles. Our system aims to save time, reduce manual work, and deliver a seamless reading experience to book lovers.
